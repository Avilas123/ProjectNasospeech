NasoSpeech

A tool to support speech pathologists in conducting assessment and tracking the progress of CLP patients.

CLP is a congenetic disorder which results in inability to produce intelligible speech.It can result in feeding problems and social isolation in children.Such children undergo a surgery in their early ages.Speech pathologists play a very important role in the life of these children.They help in correcting the speech and help them to face the society.For this,the children go through several therapy sessions that can last for many years.

Currently the assessment is done using outdated and expensive tools and methods.Nasospeech uses advances in speech technology to replace these methods and aid in conducting these assessments.
