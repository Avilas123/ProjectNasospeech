Hi!

Welcome to my homepage. My name is Abhishek Shrivastava. I am a faculty member at the Department of Design (DoD), IIT Guwahati.

I conduct courses in the domain of Interaction Design. My research interests include design for users with varying tech readiness, multimodal and assistive user interface design. Besides readings in new media design, design trends, movies, cartoons and communication design in general catch my interest too. In case you have a feeling that we share a common interest, or simply have something interesting to discuss, do drop me a line at shri@iitg.ernet.in .
